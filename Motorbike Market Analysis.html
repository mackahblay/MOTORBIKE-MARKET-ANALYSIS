from sklearn.metrics import mean_squared_error, mean_absolute_error, r2_score
import math
def evaluator(pred):
    print(f'mean squared error: {round(mean_squared_error(y_test, pred), 6)}')
    print(f'root mean squared error: {round(math.sqrt(mean_squared_error(y_test, pred)),6)}')
    print(f'mean absolute error: {round(mean_absolute_error(y_test, pred), 6)}')
    print(f'adjusted r squared: {round(r2_score(y_test, pred), 6)}')

MODEL CONCLUSION

So fuel type is the feature with the most importance to price of a motorbike which makes sense because most riders have some form of gas bikes and are not ready to switch to any form of electric bikes.

At a closer look at the data, electric bikes were not that popular in the motorbike market

The feature that came in second by a considerably wide margin from the first is the gear type of the car. Majority of bikers in everyday life ride a manual and it would take quite some time for them to switch over to semi-automatic or fully automatic bikes

My takeaway from this project with regard to Motorbike sales is to prioritize the fuel type and the gear type in order to make more sales. Specifically, you should aim at having significantly more gas based and manual motorbikes at the dealership, shop or on the website where you sell your motorbikes. Doing these will ensure you reach your revenue and profit quotas!
